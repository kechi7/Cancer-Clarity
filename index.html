Our Mission

A disproportionate amount of women of color are passing away from skin cancer as a result of misdiagnosis. Through data visualization, we hope to bring awereness of disparities to the world of cancer, specificilly for women of color, and to spark up implementation of equitable health practices. We want to make sure that everyone has access to the information they need to be informed. With the statistics and data, highlighting the disproportionate numbers in incidence rates, health professionals may find more evidence of the root cause of these discrepancies through a holistic review.
